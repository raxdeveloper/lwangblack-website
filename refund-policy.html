REFUND POLICY

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026

1. Our Guarantee

We take pride in the quality of Lwang Black clove-infused coffee. If your product arrives damaged or you received the incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order, and we will make it right.

2. Eligibility for Returns

  • Item must be unused and in the original packaging.
  • Since coffee is a perishable food item, we cannot accept returns on opened coffee products simply due to a change in taste preference.
  • Non-coffee items (apparel, accessories) can be returned within 14 days if unworn, unwashed, and in original condition.

3. Refund Process

To initiate a return or report an issue, please contact us at brewed@lwangblack.com.au or call 9857059386. Include your order number and photos of the item if it arrived damaged.

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed and credited back to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.
